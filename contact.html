Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical process for a new project?

+

I follow a 4-step process: 1) Discovery - understanding your needs through consultation, 2) Design - creating concepts and prototypes, 3) Development - building and refining your project, and 4) Delivery - finalizing and providing support for implementation.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Simple logo designs might take 3-5 days, while full websites can take 2-6 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a specific timeline based on your project scope.

Do you offer revisions on your work?

+

Yes, all projects include a set number of revision rounds to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. The specific number depends on the service package, but I'm committed to delivering work you love.

How do payments work?

+

Most projects require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon completion. For larger projects, I can arrange milestone payments. I'll provide detailed payment terms with your project proposal.

Can you work with clients internationally?

+

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and can accommodate different time zones for meetings. All communication can be done via email or video calls, making the process smooth regardless of location.
